Opportunity

Dietary supplements offer great therapeutic potential: fewer side effects, lower cost, and no need for a medical prescription compared to many traditional drugs.

The combination of massive user testimony, artificial intelligence, and structured data creates a unique opportunity to democratize knowledge about supplement effectiveness.

The Problem

  • Lack of structured, accessible data from real experiences.
  • No organized, understandable evidence for the general public.
  • No neutral platforms that separate marketing from real information.

The Solution: Suppient

Suppient is a digital platform (web and mobile) where users can:

  • Share their supplement experiences through structured questionnaires.
  • Access aggregated results on perceived effectiveness, side effects, and response times.
  • Explore an independent marketplace based on real reputation.

The platform uses AI and data analysis to identify patterns, generate visualizations, and enhance decision-making.

Target Audience

  • People seeking natural alternatives for health problems.
  • Health professionals with an integrative approach.
  • Supplement brands committed to transparency.
